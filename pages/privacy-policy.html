Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to beatCode. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our competitive programming platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you register and use beatCode:

  • Account Details: Your username/handle, email address, password, and profile information.
  • Activity Data: Code submissions, contest participation, snippets, and event rankings.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and device information to ensure platform security.

3. Use of Your Information

The data we collect is utilized to provide a seamless and robust experience:

  • To execute and judge your code algorithms accurately via Judge0.
  • To maintain global and contest-specific leaderboards.
  • To manage mass account creations for educational workshops and organizations.
  • To improve our platform, identifying and resolving runtime bugs.

4. Data Security & Contests

We implement strict security protocols to prevent unauthorized access to your account and code submissions. Specifically during active contests, solutions and discussion boards are heavily restricted or automatically hidden to maintain strict competitive integrity.

5. Third-Party Challenges

If you utilize our feature to fetch 3rd party algorithmic challenges from recognized platforms, please be aware that you are also subject to their respective privacy terms and usage guidelines.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact the অরুণ প্রাতের তরুণ দল development team via the Contact form on our home page.
